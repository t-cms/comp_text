Rep. Sylvester Turner, D-Texas, issued the following news release:
Houston, TX - Congressman Sylvester Turner, proudly serving the 18th Congressional District of Texas and a member of the Homeland Security and Science, Space, & Technology Committees, is hosting a Press Conference on President Trump's recent executive orders on Friday, January 31, 2025, at 10:00 AM (Central Time) at the Mickey Leland Federal Building, 1919 Smith St, Houston, TX 77002 (First Floor Conference Room). The event will address the recent executive orders and their impact on Houston, including topics such as immigration, Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI), equal employment opportunities, the pausing of the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA), and other pressing issues shaping local and national policies.
"Now more than ever, it is critical that we engage in meaningful conversations about the policies that shape our community and our nation," said Congressman Turner.

"Houston thrives because of its diversity and the contributions of all who work hard and enrich our city," said Congressman Turner.

The following reports were taken from the Boston University Police Department's crime logs from Jan. 10 to Jan. 17.
The Boston University Police Department. This week's crime logs include multiple trespassings, a noise complaint, and destruction of property. SARAH CRUZ/DFP PHOTOGRAPHER
Trespass at 715 Albany St.
At 4:06 p.m. on Jan. 10, a male and female, both wearing black hoodies and grey pants, were removed from the west entrance of the Talbot Building. BUPD reported narcotics were in use. 
Trespass at 72 East Concord St.
At 4:41 p.m. on Jan. 10, a male was removed from the lobby of the building after requesting to use the restroom. 
Serious medical at 68 East Concord St.
Between 10 a.m. and 11 a.m. on Jan. 13, a male was found crumpled over in front of the employee parking lot.
Noise complaint at 715 Albany St.
At 1:44 p.m. on Jan. 13, a BU employee called from the Talbot Building with a noise complaint about a silver Maserati sedan.
Hazardous condition at 650 Albany St.
At 5:45 a.m. on Jan. 14, four capped needles, saline packets and various trash were found in front of the Evans Biomedical Research Center. 
Unknown person at 121 Dummer St.
At 6:51 a.m. on Jan. 15, a caller reported an unknown party sleeping in the basement of the building. BUPD made contact with the party in the lower level of the garage, and the party moved. 
Trespass at 815 Albany St.
At 12:55 p.m. on Jan. 16, a male party wearing all black and riding a black bike was looking through car windows in the parking lot. The male party was removed. 
Destruction of property at 930 Commonwealth Ave.
At 2:23 p.m. on Jan. 16, a caller reported a man scraped a rainbow flag sticker off of her office door. The suspect was 5 feet 5 inches tall and wearing a brown coat. 
Unknown person at 590 Commonwealth Ave.
At 2:36 p.m. on Jan. 16, BUPD received a report of an unknown person sleeping in the lobby of the Metcalf Center for Science and Engineering.
Breaking and entering at 722 Commonwealth Ave.
Between 11 a.m. and 11:06 a.m. on Jan. 17, a caller reported an unknown party entered their unlocked residence. The party left without identifying themself. 
Service request at 175 Cambridge St.
At 2:04 p.m. on Jan 17, BUPD received a call from the president's office at the above location about teen students crossing the frozen pond. State police were notified.
Suspicious vehicle at 194 Bay State Road
At 8:05 p.m. on Jan. 17, a caller reported their Uber driver's vehicle remaining outside of the building 40 minutes after they were dropped off at the above location.

Disabled federal workers fear for their jobs after Trump remarks

Trump’s comments, where he claimed without evidence the agency is “actively recruiting workers who suffer severe intellectual disabilities and psychiatric problems,” has sparked anxiety and frustration among federal workers with disabilities, many of whom are military veterans.
